If you no longer want to continue using ORCID you can deactivate your account at any time.

Once your account is deactivated your ORCID iD and record will be disabled. All data, apart from the primary email address, is deleted. The email address is cryptographically hashed and securely stored in a private data file.

You will no longer be able to use your iD to sign in or access other services. Your public record remains accessible but will only contain the iD and a ‘Deactivated record’ message.

Learn more about deactivating an ORCID account

If you have accidentally created multiple ORCID accounts you can always remove the duplicates instead of deactivating them. Account settings > Remove duplicate record
